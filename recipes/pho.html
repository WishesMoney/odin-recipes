Rough Ingredients

(Reagents for you witches following at home)

  • Beef Soup Bones

    • Use the shank and knee to make the homemade bone broth brothy enough.

  • Rice Noodles

    • You should be able to find dry rice noodles at most grocery stores, unless they're slacking.

  • Beef

    • Beef top sirloin, thinly sliced. Do Chef Ramsey proud.

  • Fish Sauce

    • For a salty finish, and fishy flavor.

  • Onion

    • Roast the onions for depth and breadth of flavor.

  • Spices and Seasonings

    • Ginger, star anise, salt, pizzazz, panache, and a hint of gumption.

  • Garnishes

    • Cilantro, green onion, Thai basil, lime, hoisin sauce, sriracha, and anything else.

HOW TO PREPARE RIGHT NOW?

  1. Roast Bones and Onion

    2. Roast the beef bones and onions until the bones are browned and the onions are soft. No 'ard onions need apply.

  2. Make the Broth

    3. Combine roasted beef bones, roasted onions, ginger, salt, star anise, fish sauce, and water in a large stockpot. Simmer that bad boy for 6 to 10 hours. Take no gruff from the cow. Strain into a new saucepan.

  3. Soak the Rice Noodles

    4. Soak those noodles in water for 1 hour. Meanwhile, bring the broth to a simmer. Take no prisoners.

  4. Pray

    5. Because at this point we should all be in over our heads.
